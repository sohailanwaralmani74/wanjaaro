The Budget Formula

Understanding your budget is simple math:

Net Balance = Total Income − Total Expenses Where: Total Income = sum of all income sources, Total Expenses = sum of all spending categories
Savings Rate = (Net Balance / Total Income) × 100 Where: Net Balance = income minus expenses, Total Income = total earnings

For example, if your total income is $6,000 and your total expenses are $4,500, your net balance is $1,500 and your savings rate is 25%.
