GST Calculation Formula – How It Works

Understanding the gst calculation formula helps you verify results and appreciate how the math works. This gst calc uses two standard formulas depending on the mode:

Add GST (Tax‑Exclusive):
GST Amount = Base Amount × (Rate / 100)
Total = Base Amount + GST Amount
Remove GST (Tax‑Inclusive):
GST Amount = Total × (Rate / (100 + Rate))
Base Amount = Total – GST Amount

This gst tax calculator applies these formulas automatically, so you never have to do manual calculations. Whether you're checking an invoice or preparing a BAS statement, the gst calculation formula ensures you get the correct numbers every time.
